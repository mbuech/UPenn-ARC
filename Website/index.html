Leadership

Titles and Titles

The ARC UPenn Chapter is currently led by students interested in all sides of the Math-Finance-Computer Science combination that Quantitative Trading entails.

  • Spriha Baruah

    Global ARC Co-Founder and President

    M.S in Computer and Information Technology (UPenn '15), and B.A in Economics (UPenn '15). Together with Nate (Harvard '15), she leads the Global ARC organization. At UPenn, she works with Ryan to lead the UPenn ARC chapter and with Matt to lead the UPenn ARC Computer-Science effort. While not at ARC, she can be found hacking on side-projects, swimming, playing the ukulele, and/or wearing chopsticks.

  • Ryan Farell

    UPenn ARC Co-Founder and President

    B.S. in Economics (Finance concentration) (UPenn '15). Ryan leads the UPenn ARC together with Spriha and leads all Math-related efforts at ARC. He develops Math-related educational resources at ARC for members interested in the learning more about Quantitative Finance. While not at ARC, he can be found playing Chess, rowing on the UPenn Lightweight Crew Team, and learning new things online.

  • Matthew Buechler

    UPenn ARC Co-Founder and Head of Computer Science

    B.S.E in Networked and Social Systems Engineering (NETS) (UPenn '15). As a Co-Founder of the UPenn ARC, Matt leads everything Computer-Science related at the club and develops educational resources for members interested in learning Computer-Science applicable to Quantitative Finance. While not at ARC, you can find him hacking on websites and large data sets, or working on his technology consulting business
